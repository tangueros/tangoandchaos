Most people go to the Chacarita Cemetery to see Carlos Gardel's tomb, but for tango dancers there's an even more interesting place. I found it on my bike one day when I was taking a shortcut through the cemetery on my way home from Parque Sarmiento. It may be the most interesting tango location in Buenos Aires, but I've never seen it written about or discussed. For those who want to make the trip, I marked it on this Google Map image.
